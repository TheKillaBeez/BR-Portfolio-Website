Public Relations Capstone Project

This page is dedicated to my senior public relations capstone project. Together with 3 other students, we formed Maryland Susans Public Relations and were given the opportunity to work with the Baltimore Furniture Bank. A non-profit focused on providing gently used, refurbished furniture to the recently homeless or unhoused community across the DMV. As a team we developed a 4 month long PR campaign and developed multiple pieces of media and infastructure for the organization.

One of the first deliverables our team created that I was responsible for creating was the warehouse signage. I created 32 total designs in the beginning and 16 that are officially in use at their warehouse today.

One of the biggest and most important deliverables during our campaign was creating a new brochure for the BFB. This was something that our team leader: Liberty Diaz, and I got to work on together. It was created with both case workers, clients, and partners in mind and we wanted something the BFB could be proud to display.
